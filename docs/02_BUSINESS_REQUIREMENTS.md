# 02. Business Requirements

## Target Audience
- Proposal Teams
- Sales Engineers
- Bid Managers

## Key Features
- **Document Ingestion**: Upload RFPs (PDF, DOCX, XLSX).
- **Knowledge Base Retrieval**: Semantically search past proposals, product wikis, and case studies.
- **Drafting Engine**: Generate contextual proposal responses per RFP question.
- **Compliance Matrix**: Automatically map and verify compliance against RFP criteria.
- **Review and Edit Interface**: Allow human-in-the-loop review, editing, and approval of generated responses.
